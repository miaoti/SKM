Configure storefront filter for product metafield `custom.fits_vehicles`:

1. In Shopify Admin, open Search & Discovery.
2. Go to Filters, add a product metafield filter.
3. Choose namespace `custom`, key `fits_vehicles`.
4. Save and ensure the filter appears on collection pages.
5. Verify URL filtering works with `?filter.p.m.custom.fits_vehicles=<vehicle_gid>`.
